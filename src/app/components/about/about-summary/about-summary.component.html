Summary

This application was made for the course 'Clientside web frameworks' at Avans Hogeschool Breda.

CSWP Angular Front-end

This Front-end serves provides a place for end-users to put up their belongs for sale, akin to Tweakers.net vraag en aanbod & Marktplaats. You can create advertisements on which people can comment and bid. You can only edit or delete comments or advertisements if they are your own. You can only bid on items they're on other people's advertisements. However, you can comments on other people's threads as well as reply to other people's comments. You also have the ability to like or dislike (or upvote/downvote) comments.You have a user profile which you can edit. You can change your password, for example.
